# 📊 Moooscle (MSCL) — Tokenomics

This document provides a clear and transparent overview of the Moooscle (MSCL)
token distribution, supply structure, and the philosophy behind its economics.

---

## 🧮 Total Supply

**Total Supply:** 1,000,000,000,000 MSCL  
**Decimals:** 18  
**Supply Type:** Fixed — cannot be increased or decreased  
**Minting:** Not possible  
**Burning (function):** Not implemented  

The total supply value stored in the contract is immutable and matches the
verified on-chain source.

---

## 🔥 Community‑Driven Burning

The contract does **not** implement a `burn()` function, meaning the total
supply cannot be programmatically reduced by the contract itself.

However, **voluntary community‑driven burning** is always possible by sending
tokens to the standard dead address:

`0x000000000000000000000000000000000000dEaD`

Tokens sent to this address are permanently inaccessible and effectively
removed from circulation.  
If the community decides that a burn event is beneficial for the project,
tokens can be burned through this method in a transparent and verifiable way.

> The total supply stored in the contract does not change,  
> but the **circulating supply** decreases.

## 📦 Distribution Breakdown

The Moooscle supply is divided into **five main categories**, designed to
support long‑term growth, fairness, and community involvement.

### 1. Liquidity Pool — 30%

- Added to the POL–MSCL pool on Polygon  
- Ensures trading stability and accessibility  
- No presale, no private sale, no privileged wallets  

### 2. Community (Released Over Time) — 30%

- Rewards for early believers  
- Rituals, challenges, XP systems  
- Distributed gradually to avoid inflation or dumping  
- No classic airdrops, no bot farming  

### 3. Collaborations / Utility / Game — 20%

- Future integrations  
- Game mechanics  
- Cross‑project collaborations  
- Utility expansions  

### 4. Treasury — 10%

- Long‑term sustainability  
- Strategic reserves  
- Emergency operations  
- No spending without community visibility  

### 5. Team (Guideline Category) — 10%

- Not a privileged wallet  
- No locked supply, no hidden allocations  
- Evolves with community governance  
- Symbolic category for future structured roles

- No contract‑level burn function
