# 🐮 Moooscle — Project Overview

Moooscle is a community‑driven movement built on transparency, discipline, and
identity.  
It is not just a token — it is a character, a story, and a shared journey where
everyone starts equal and grows through participation.

---

## 🌐 What Moooscle Represents

Moooscle is a symbol of:

- **strength**  
- **consistency**  
- **self‑improvement**  
- **community belonging**  
- **playful identity**  

The project blends crypto culture with character‑driven storytelling, creating a
universe where the mascot evolves together with the community.

---

## 🧩 Core Principles

### **1. Transparency**
No presale, no private sale, no privileged wallets, no hidden mechanics.

### **2. Discipline**
Growth is earned through rituals, participation, and engagement — not shortcuts.

### **3. Community First**
The community decides the direction, the rituals, and the evolution of Moooscle.

### **4. Simplicity**
The token is immutable, safe, and free from unnecessary complexity.

---

## 🪙 The Token (MSCL)

- Network: Polygon PoS  
- Standard: ERC‑20  
- Supply: 1,000,000,000,000 MSCL  
- No mint  
- No contract‑level burn  
- No taxes  
- No owner privileges  

The token is designed to be **simple, fair, and immutable**.

---

## 🔥 Community‑Driven Evolution

Moooscle grows through:

- challenges  
- rituals  
- XP systems  
- community decisions  
- creative expansions  
- collaborations  

The community can also decide to perform **manual burn events** by sending tokens
to the dead address, reducing circulating supply in a transparent way.
