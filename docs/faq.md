# ❓ Moooscle — Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

This FAQ provides clear answers to the most common questions about Moooscle,  
the MSCL token, and the philosophy behind the project.

---

## 🐮 What is Moooscle?

Moooscle is the mascot of **revenge, discipline, and unshakable self‑belief**.  
It represents the moment when people try to push you down, when they tell you  
*“you won’t make it”*, when they doubt you, underestimate you, or hope you fail —  
and you keep going anyway.

Moooscle is the symbol of **rising back up**, training harder, proving yourself,  
and believing in your own strength even when no one else does.

It embodies the desire for **redemption**, the hunger for **improvement**,  
and the courage to trust yourself beyond every doubt.

Moooscle is not just a token —  
it is a character, a story, and a shared identity for everyone who refuses to give up.

---

## 🪙 What is the MSCL token?

MSCL is the official ERC‑20 token of the Moooscle project, deployed on Polygon PoS.  
It is simple, immutable, and free from hidden mechanics.

Key properties:

- fixed supply  
- no mint  
- no contract‑level burn  
- no taxes  
- no owner privileges  
- no blacklist  

---

## 🔥 Can Moooscle be burned?

Yes — but only through **community‑driven manual burning**.

The contract does **not** include a `burn()` function, but anyone can burn tokens  
by sending them to the dead address:

`0x000000000000000000000000000000000000dEaD`

This reduces the **circulating supply**, not the total supply stored in the contract.

---

## 🧱 Is the contract safe?

Yes.  
The contract is fully immutable and contains:

- no upgradeability  
- no proxy  
- no privileged functions  
- no owner‑controlled mechanics  
- no external calls  
- no hidden fees  

It is a standard ERC‑20 implementation.

## 💰 Was there a presale or private sale?

No.  
Moooscle launched with:

- no presale  
- no private sale  
- no early insiders  
- no privileged wallets  

Everyone starts equal.

## 🧩 How is the supply distributed?

The supply is divided into:

- 30% Liquidity  
- 30% Community (released over time)  
- 20% Collaborations / Utility / Game  
- 10% Treasury  
- 10% Team (symbolic, no privileged wallet)  

Full details are in `TOKENOMICS.md`.

## 🎨 Who controls the Moooscle mascot?

The mascot is part of the Moooscle brand identity.  
Community art and adaptations are welcome as long as they follow the rules in  
`brand.md`:

- no human skin  
- no extra limbs  
- no realistic textures  
- no horror or gore  
- no copyrighted logos  

---

## 📈 Is Moooscle a financial project?

No.  
Moooscle is an identity‑driven, community‑driven project.  
There are:

- no promises  
- no financial incentives  
- no yield  
- no staking  
- no rewards tied to price  

The value of Moooscle is in the **community**, not speculation.
